Consider the majesty and wonder of God, in creation, in relationships, in power, in love; allow the mystery of God to cause you to marvel and worship.

A Prayer to Our Triune God

Heavenly Father, you anointed your Son Jesus Christ with the Holy Spirit and with power to bring to us the blessings of your kingdom. Anoint your Church and children with the same Holy Spirit, that we who share in his suffering and victory may bear witness to the gospel of salvation; through Jesus Christ, your Son our Lord, who is alive and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen.
